BIM

Building Information Modelling (BIM) is the process of representing building information in a 3D format, usually along with the geometry. This representation is more intuitive than a table in a database and allows huge savings in time and increases efficiency.

One Model

One model is shared among all the stakeholders, from Architects to Civil and MEP Engineers to Contractors, Owners and Fabricators. Maximize Accuracy. Minimize re-work.

3D +

BIM is more than just a 3D model. Extra layers of information can be added over the geometry like Project Scheduling, Estimation, Facility Management as well as tracking Energy expenditure and Sustainability.

BIM as a service

Want the benefits of BIM but do not want to invest in the infrastructure ? We provide BIM as a service so you can get high-quality IFC-compliant BIM. Get in touch.
